The Salon Story

Founded in 2012, The Hair Room was built on a simple philosophy: hair should be personal, wearable, and healthy. For over a decade, we have been a sanctuary in Earlsdon.

Every client is a collaborator. Every consultation is a conversation. Every service is bespoke.
